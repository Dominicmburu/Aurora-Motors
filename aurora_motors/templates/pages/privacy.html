1. Overview

{{ COMPANY_NAME }} ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our car rental services, website, mobile applications, and related services (collectively, the "Service").

This policy applies to all information collected by {{ COMPANY_NAME }} and covers both personal information you provide directly to us and information we collect automatically through your use of our Service.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information You Provide

We collect information you provide directly to us, including:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, phone number, date of birth, address
  • Driver Information: Driver's license number, license history, driving record
  • Payment Information: Credit card details, billing address, payment history
  • Booking Information: Reservation details, preferences, special requests
  • Identity Verification: Government-issued ID, passport, proof of address
  • Communication Data: Messages, support tickets, feedback, survey responses
2.2 Information Collected Automatically

When you use our Service, we automatically collect:

  • Device Information: IP address, browser type, operating system, device identifiers
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, time spent, click patterns, search queries
  • Location Data: GPS coordinates, pickup/drop-off locations (with your consent)
  • Vehicle Data: Mileage, fuel consumption, vehicle diagnostics (where applicable)
  • Cookies and Tracking: Session data, preferences, analytics information
2.3 Information from Third Parties

We may receive information about you from:

  • Credit reporting agencies and identity verification services
  • Insurance companies and claims processors
  • Business partners and referral sources
  • Social media platforms (with your permission)
  • Government agencies (for compliance purposes)
