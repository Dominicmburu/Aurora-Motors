About Aurora Motors

Our Story

Founded in 2020, Aurora Motors has quickly become Australia's premier car rental service. We started with a simple mission: to provide customers with access to premium vehicles and exceptional service at competitive prices.

Today, we serve thousands of customers across Australia with our diverse fleet of well-maintained vehicles and commitment to customer satisfaction.

Our Mission

To provide reliable, premium car rental services that exceed customer expectations while maintaining the highest standards of safety and environmental responsibility.

Our Vision

To be Australia's most trusted and innovative car rental company, leading the industry in customer satisfaction and sustainable transportation solutions.
